A Vietnamese-English dictionary app with AI-powered etymology and example generation.

Features

  • Vietnamese-English and English-Vietnamese dictionary lookup
  • AI-generated example sentences
  • Etymology exploration with Wiktionary integration
  • Save and organize words
  • Search history
  • Import and export saved words

FAQ

Is the app free?
Yes, the app is free to use.
Does the app require an internet connection?
The dictionary works offline. An internet connection is only needed for AI-generated examples and etymology lookups.
What languages are supported?
The app supports Vietnamese-English and English-Vietnamese translations.

Privacy

từ điển does not collect or share any personal data. All saved words and search history are stored locally on your device.
