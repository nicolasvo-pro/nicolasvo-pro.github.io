từ điển - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

từ điển is a Vietnamese-English dictionary app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

từ điển does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require an account or login.

Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Saved words
  • Search history

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the app's export feature.

Network Requests

The app connects to external services for the following optional features:

  • AI-generated examples and etymology: When you request an example sentence or etymology, the word is sent to an AI service to generate the content. No personal information is included in these requests.
  • Wiktionary: The app fetches etymology data from Wiktionary's public API.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
